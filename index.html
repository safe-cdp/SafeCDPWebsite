What's SafeCDP?

SafeCDP allows CDP owners to manage their position in a totally hands-off manner.
Where traditional CDP managers fall short, SafeCDP steps up to the plate. By transferring your CDP to our CDP Manager i.e. smart contract, we are able to handle your position in a decentralized and trustless way.

In SafeCDP, if the value of your collateral falls below a certain threshold, you are given a "margin call", i.e. a notification to add more collateral or repay some debt. As long as you respond to the margin call within a given period of time, nothing can happen to your collateral. Your collateral is only sold if you fail to respond to your margin call. If your CDP was never in any danger of liquidation, then you don't have to pay anything.

This means it's almost always a good idea to secure your CDP with SafeCDP, since it resembles an insurance plan with no upfront cost (beyond gas fees).
